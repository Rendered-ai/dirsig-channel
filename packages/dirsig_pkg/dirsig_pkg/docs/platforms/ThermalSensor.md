Configurable resolution camera operating in the thermal infrared wavelength range;

Simulates thermal imaging with adjustable pixel pitch and focal length;

Configurable mount orientation with two options:
- Down (default): Nadir viewing with rotation angles (0,0,0) - sensor points straight down
- Forward: Forward-looking with rotation angles (90,0,0) - sensor points forward in the direction of travel

Capable of collecting location, material, and temperature truth data;

Configurable frame rate and integration time;

Can be mounted on various platforms and supports flexible motion through the Flex Motion input.
